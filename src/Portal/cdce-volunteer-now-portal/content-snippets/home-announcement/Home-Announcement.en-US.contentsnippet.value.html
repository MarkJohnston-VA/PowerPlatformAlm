Prepare for VA’s Secure Sign-In Changes

In 2025, you'll have two secure sign-in account options (Login.gov and ID.me) for all VA websites and apps. We removed the My HealtheVet option on March 5, 2025. We'll remove the DS Logon option after September 30, 2025. You'll still be able to use your My HealtheVet health portal - you'll just need to sign in to it with a Login.gov or ID.me account.
